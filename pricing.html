Pricing


These base prices are meant as a guideline only.

For an exact price, you will need a quote on your design.
We only give out quotes when we are open for new commissions.

Please follow us on social media, or check the homepage often to see when we are open.


CUSTOM TAILS
$50 - $500
Any species, any design!
Prices vary based on complexity.
Options include: spikes, feathers, lights, stripes, spots, curls, hidden pockets, and more!

HANDPAWS
$150 - $250
Options include four or five fingers, monster claw paws, monkey hands, sparkle vinyl pawpads, shiny PVC pawpads, resin claws, vinyl claws, "puffy" fingers, and more!

FEETPAWS
$250 - $350
Options include indoor or outdoor bottoms, sockpaws, clawpaws, monkey feet, sparkle vinyl pawpads, shiny PVC pawpads, resin claws, vinyl claws, and more!

FURSUIT HEAD
$800 - $1500
Any species, any design!
All our heads have follow-me eyes, moving jaws, and are machine washable.
Options include: spikes, horns, feathers, lights, plush or resin teeth, interchangeable eyes, tongues, hair and more!

PARTIAL FURSUIT
$1100 - $2500
The most basic partial is a head, handpaws, and a tail.
A deluxe partial might include arm sleeves, feet, leg sleeves, wings, and upper half or lower half body.

FULL FURSUIT
$1700 - $6000
A full fursuit leaves no skin exposed for the most complete animal illusion!

Options include: digitigrade padding, wings, hidden pockets, front or back zipper, and more!
